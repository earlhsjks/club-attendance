Data Privacy Notice and Terms of Use

This system is established for the purpose of recording and displaying club attendance records within the Computer Studies Department.

The system contains student information collected by authorized club officers, limited to the following: Full Name, School ID Number, Course, Year Level, and Attendance Records. These data are processed solely for attendance verification, internal documentation, and department-related reporting. No personal data is collected or used for commercial, marketing, or external distribution purposes.

Prohibited: Access to this system is strictly limited to viewing one’s own record only. Any attempt to access, search, or view another individual’s information, as well as any unauthorized copying, sharing, reproduction, or distribution of data, is strictly prohibited.

Information displayed in this system is provided for informational purposes and does not constitute an official academic or institutional record.

Personal data are retained for the duration of the current academic year, unless continued retention is required for legitimate academic or organizational purposes. Requests for correction of inaccurate or incomplete information may be submitted to authorized club or department officers. Users are not permitted to directly modify stored records.

Data Removal: Students may submit a request for the removal of their personal data. Such requests shall be reviewed and acted upon when the data is no longer necessary for legitimate academic or organizational purposes. Where immediate removal is not feasible, the data may be restricted or archived in accordance with applicable policies.

By proceeding, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agreed to this Data Privacy Notice and Terms of Use, and that you accept responsibility for using the system strictly within its intended purpose and limitations.
